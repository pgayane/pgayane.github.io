Hello everyone, I am Gayane Petrosyan

I am currently in Hacker School, in New York City. Hacker School is one of my best experiences as a programmer. Here in Hacker School I met around 60 programmers from around the world with big variety of experience and expertise. This gives you a chance to learn basically anything with the help of other hacker schoolers and of course amazing hacker school facilitators. Besides we have complete freedom of choosing what to learn and what projects to do. One can decide to learn a new funky programming language, reimplement a regex engine or bittorrent client, give more presentations, pair program, get code reviewed and etc. you have got an idea. In short: Hacker School helps me to become better programmer and also to become more confident and self-aware of my abilities.
